W E B D E V E L O P E R

Nice to meet your acquaintance, I'm Molly Porphura. As a professional graphic designer, I've always had a critical eye for visuals, which is why I decided to become a front-end software developer. Since I was a child, I've always hated the terribly designed user interfaces of the time, so as an adult, I've come to believe that I have an obligation as a great asset to apply my skills to improve upon anything I can. All my previous work was simply a way to get me to a spot where I could. Not to say I didn't gain any skills along the way; in fact, that's exactly what I did: Project Management, Working on Deadlines, Working with a Team. All skills I will apply in any future positions I take.
